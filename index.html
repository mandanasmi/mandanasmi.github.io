About Me


My full name is Mandana Samiei. I born on 16th of June, 1994 at Tehran, Iran. Mandana is my first name that is taken from a heroic figure of Iran history and tradition. The name “Mandana” means "Eternal” in Persian. Mandana was a princess of Media and, later, the Queen consort of Cambyses I of Anshan and mother of Cyrus the Great ruler of the Persia's Achaemenid Dynasty (the 1st Persian Emperor and the writer of the first declaration of human rights in the globe, named The Cyrus Cylinder).
